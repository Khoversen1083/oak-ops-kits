# The 6-Layer Research Brief

## What you're building

A one-page brief on every A-tier prospect before you write the email. Ten minutes per brief. Turns generic outreach into a message that sounds like you already know their business.

## Before you start

- Locked niche and A-tier list (top 8 from ICP scoring)
- Apollo or scrape data for contact basics
- LinkedIn open
- The 6-Layer Brief template from Resources

## Steps

1. **Layer 1 - Account overview.** Legal name, HQ, independent vs subsidiary, rough revenue band, headcount, ownership status.
2. **Layer 2 - Current systems.** What are they running today? Old ERP, spreadsheets, QuickBooks, paper logs. Note where you learned it (job posting, careers page, review).
3. **Layer 3 - Key contacts.** One or two people worth reaching. Name, title, LinkedIn, warm-path flag (mutual connections, same school, shared past employer).
4. **Layer 4 - Recent news.** Anything from the last 12-18 months. Move, expansion, leadership hire, stated initiative, award. One real event gives you a reason to reach out now.
5. **Layer 5 - Buying signals.** Job postings hinting at manual process or tooling gaps. Growth that outran their systems. Founder posts about being underwater.
6. **Layer 6 - Talking points.** Three to five specific lines: one tied to recent event, one to current systems, one to a known pain, one warm-path angle.
7. Batch it. Build all 8 briefs in one sitting, then write all 8 emails in the next.
