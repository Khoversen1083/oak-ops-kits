# The 25 to 500 Target Escalation System

## What you're building

A four-stage list system that scales outreach from 25 hand-picked targets to a 500-target machine, without burning your sender reputation or your Apollo credits.

## Before you start

- Locked niche and ICP rubric
- One email account warmed at least two weeks
- Apollo or equivalent enrichment access
- The 25 / 150 / 200 / 500 List worksheets downloaded from Resources

## Steps

1. **Stage 1 - 25 List (this week).** Hand-pick 25 perfect-fit owners. You wrote each hook yourself. Two minutes of research per owner. Goal: prove you can write a cold email that gets one reply.
2. **Stage 2 - 150 List (weeks 2 to 3).** Same niche, broader geography. Apollo or Google Maps does the company pull. You still write each hook (or 3 to 5 templates with field swaps). Goal: prove the email is reply-worthy at volume.
3. **Stage 3 - 200 List (week 4 once proof exists).** Add the ICP Scoring Calculator. Cut anything below A or B tier. Run through Instantly. Goal: predictable reply rate, 2 per 100 sent floor.
4. **Stage 4 - 500 List (month 2+).** Full enrichment (Apollo verified + Million Verifier). Tier by ICP: A (top 50 custom), B (next 150 light personalization), C (300 sequence-only). Goal: 10 booked calls per month.
5. Each tier has its own worksheet. Never skip ahead. Each stage teaches you what reply hooks work for your niche.
