# Lead Acquisition Playbook

The 9-lesson sequence I run when I open a new market. Comes from 7 years of
enterprise SaaS sales, ATVs from $25M to $50M, calling on CIOs and CMOs who
do not take cold calls. Same playbook, simpler scale.

Read it in order. Each lesson is one page. Each one has a matching kit under
`../kits/` that runs the system for you.

## The 9 lessons

1. [Lock the niche](01-niche-selection.md) — pick the right one, do not skip
2. [ICP scoring](02-icp-scoring.md) — who inside the niche is worth contacting
3. [Scale from 25 to 200 targets](03-scale-targets-25-to-200.md) — the volume
   ramp that does not break quality
4. [Source leads with Apollo and Google Maps](04-source-leads-apollo-google-maps.md)
   — where the 200 actually come from
5. [Prospect research brief](05-prospect-research-brief.md) — 6 layers, 4
   minutes, the brief I walk into every call with
6. [The 3-sentence cold email](06-3-sentence-cold-email.md) — the hook, the
   proof, the ask
7. [Follow-up sequence](07-follow-up-sequence.md) — 3 touches, days 3, 8, 14,
   breakup email last
8. [Decision tree per reply type](08-decision-tree-replies.md) — what to send
   when they say "interested", "not now", "send info", "remove me"
9. [Pipeline tracker](09-pipeline-tracker.md) — one CSV, daily standup, weekly
   review, "pipeline status" from any folder

## How to use it

Read all 9 once. Pick the lesson you are stuck on this week. Open the
matching kit folder. Drop it into your Claude Code workspace. Tell Claude
"run the kit". The kit interviews you, integrates into your repo, runs the
system.
