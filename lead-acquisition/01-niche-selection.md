# Lock Your Niche

## What you're building

A locked niche you can ship outreach to this week. One sentence specific enough that you can name 25 real businesses without thinking.

## Before you start

- A free afternoon (90 minutes max)
- LinkedIn open
- The Niche Picker worksheet downloaded from Resources below
- A bias toward picking something boring and owner-operated

## Steps

1. Pick three candidate niches. Be specific. "Construction" is too broad. "Concrete pumping companies, Pacific Northwest, $30M to $100M, family-owned" is the right specificity.
2. Run each candidate against the 5-filter rubric in the Niche Picker. Pass = green flag, fail = drop it.
3. For your top candidate, search `[industry] software` and `[industry] ERP`. 1990s websites and Excel marketplaces = green flag.
4. Search `Owner` or `President` plus the industry plus a city. If you can find 100 profiles in five minutes, sourcing is doable.
5. Skim the first five owner profiles. Slammed, working weekends, hiring problems, ops headaches = your pain hypothesis is real.
6. Open three random company sites. Manual-labor careers pages + a single contact email = owner-operated. Lock it.
7. Write your locked niche as one sentence at the top of the worksheet. Stop researching. Move to ICP scoring.
