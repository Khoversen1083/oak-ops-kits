# Example First Setter Hire Spec

Generated for the vet-clinic operator, projected for use at week 14 of the 12-week plan (when client count and cash will allow). This is what `outputs/setter-spec-<date>.md` looks like after Step 6.

Date: 2026-05-30 (drafted for later activation)

Status: PARKED. Operator currently has 3 clients and 7,200 cash. Hire trigger requires 5+ clients and 3+ months of fixed costs (7,200 minimum). Both thresholds projected to clear by week 14 of the current plan.

## Role: Part-time setter, vet-clinic niche

### What they do

- Pull 50 named multi-doctor vet clinic accounts per week, 3-8 doctor range, US-based
- Find owner first name and email via Apollo or AnyMailFinder
- Send 50 cold emails per week using the cold-email kit's template, scoped to the vet-clinic offer
- Run the 3-touch follow-up sequence (Day 3, Day 8, Day 14 breakup)
- Triage replies, route qualified ones to operator's Calendly
- Log every prospect into the pipeline tracker

### What they do NOT touch

- Discovery calls (operator runs all)
- Pricing or proposals (operator only)
- Delivery, kickoff, monitoring (operator only)
- Refunds, scope changes, anything post-sale
- Strategy on niche or offer

### Definition of "qualified call"

- Shows up to the booked Calendly slot
- Is an owner-operator or decision-maker at a 3-8 doctor vet clinic, US-based
- Answers the 6 discovery questions (see `../discovery-call/resources/six-question-framework.md`) without dodging

### Comp model (Option A, per booked qualified call)

- 60 per booked qualified call (sits in the 50-75 range)
- No commission on no-shows, but a credit toward the next booked call
- Cap at 20 booked calls per month
- Paid weekly via Mercury, no salary, no contractor day-rate, no signing bonus

Operator will switch to Option B (5 percent of closed setup fee) after the setter has booked 15+ qualified calls and operator has closed at least 3.

### Where to recruit

In order:

1. Vet industry insiders, former practice manager looking for part-time work, or veterinary-industry sales reps who want a side rep
2. RepVue or Bravado posts targeting part-time commission-only SDRs
3. Upwork or Contra with a paid 1-week test task (10 named accounts + 10 cold emails + 1 outreach campaign report)
4. Operator's network: anyone in the existing client base who knows a salesperson tired of full-time

Avoid: full-time SDRs from BigCo, "growth marketers", agency-style retainers.

### Week 1 spec (the trial)

- Day 1: 60-minute onboarding call, walk through the cold-email kit, voice rules, niche definition, ICP
- Day 2: setter pulls first 10 named accounts, operator reviews
- Day 3-5: setter sends first 25 emails, operator reads first 5 in real time and edits voice
- Day 6: setter sends remaining 25
- Day 7: review call, what worked, what to change for week 2

### 30-day trial structure

- Pay commission per the model above
- Day 15: check booked calls trend (target: 3 by end of week 2, 5 by end of week 4)
- Day 30: decide continue, restructure, or end
- End with no drama if not working. Pay earned commission, no severance, no negotiation.

### What the setter needs from the operator

- Locked niche definition (multi-doctor vet clinics, 3-8 docs, US-based, owner-operator)
- Documented offer (1,500 setup + 600 retainer, outcome: cut admin time 4-6 hours per week per clinic)
- Access to Instantly, Apollo, AnyMailFinder, operator's calendar
- 30-minute weekly check-in (Friday)
- Copy of `../cold-email/CLAUDE.md` for voice rules
- The vet-clinic-specific email examples from operator's last 6 months of outreach

### Decision gates before activation

The operator MUST clear these before activating this spec:

1. Client count >= 5 paying retainers (currently 3, projected by week 11 of plan)
2. Operating cash >= 7,200 (3 months of 2,400 fixed costs)
3. The setter cost (~800/month) does not push post-hire runway below 3 months

Run the cash flow model (`../../resources/cash-flow-model.md` Formula 3) to confirm before posting the role.

### When to scale beyond first setter

- Setter consistently books 20+ qualified calls per month for 3 straight months
- Operator has 10+ clients
- Operator can document why a 2nd setter would not duplicate work

Until then, one setter. Do not over-hire.
