# Example Intake, filled

This is a worked example of an intake answered by a real-shape operator. Use it as a reference for what depth of answer the coach is looking for. Real intake-filled.md files are gitignored.

Operator profile: solo, 3 vet clinic clients, scaling to 8.

## Current state, the business

1. How many paying clients do you have right now?

3 paying. All on retainer.

2. What is your current MRR?

1,800 (Client A 600, Client B 700, Client C 500).

3. What is the total revenue last month?

3,300 (1,800 MRR + 1,500 setup fee from Client C, who came on 6 weeks ago).

4. What is your locked niche right now?

Independent multi-doctor vet clinics, 3 to 8 doctors, US-based, owner-operator runs the practice.

5. How long has the average current client been with you, in months?

Client A: 7 months. Client B: 4 months. Client C: 1.5 months. Average 4.2 months.

## Current state, the operator

6. How many hours per week do you work on the business right now?

About 32, leaning heavy on delivery (20), then sales (8), admin (4).

7. How many hours per week are you willing to work to hit the target client count?

40 hard cap. Already at the edge of what I want.

8. What is your monthly fixed cost burn?

2,400. Tools (Trigger.dev, Apollo, Instantly, Cursor, Mercury): 600. Contractor VA part-time: 800. Owner draw I take regardless: 1,000.

9. What is your current operating cash on hand?

7,200.

## Targets

10. What is your target client count in 90 days?

8 clients.

11. What is your target MRR in 90 days?

4,800 (8 clients at 600 average retainer).

12. What is the one outcome that would make the next 90 days a clear win?

8 clients on retainer, all at 500 or higher, and I am working under 40 hours per week.

## Current delivery and pricing

13. What is your standard setup fee right now?

1,500. Range is 1,200 (simpler clinics) to 1,800 (more integrations).

14. What is your standard retainer right now?

600 default. Have one at 500 (Client C, traded scope for cash flow), one at 700 (Client B, added scope).

15. Do you have a documented delivery process?

Half-baked. I have a Notion doc with kickoff checklist, no documented build template or handoff process. Improvise the rest.

## Help available

16. Do you have any contractors today?

Yes, a part-time VA (8 hours per week, 25/hr) who handles inbox triage and basic Apollo list-building.

17. Have you ever hired a setter?

No. Tried a 1-week trial with someone from Upwork last quarter, they sent 200 emails that were off-voice and burned 2 of my warm prospect lists. Killed it after week 1.

## Operating preferences

18. Anything else the coach should know?

I sleep poorly when cash is below 6,000. I will hold back hires aggressively. Also, my best client (B) referred me to 2 other vet practices in the last 30 days, so there is real pull starting in the niche.
