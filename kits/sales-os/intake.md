# Sales OS Kit Intake

Answer in your own words. Two sentences each is fine. The coach reads this once and personalizes every step. Save your answers to `intake-filled.md` (not committed).

## Current state, the business

1. How many paying clients do you have right now? Setup-only counts as zero, retainer counts as one.
2. What is your current MRR (sum of all active retainers)?
3. What is the total revenue last month (retainers plus any setup fees collected)?
4. What is your locked niche right now? Industry and buyer profile in one sentence.
5. How long has the average current client been with you, in months?

## Current state, the operator

6. How many hours per week do you work on the business right now (delivery plus sales plus admin)?
7. How many hours per week are you willing to work to hit the target client count? Cap, not aspiration.
8. What is your monthly fixed cost burn (tools, contractors, rent if applicable)?
9. What is your current operating cash on hand (business account balance you can deploy)?

## Targets

10. What is your target client count in 90 days? Be specific.
11. What is your target MRR in 90 days? Math should match (target clients times average retainer).
12. What is the one outcome that would make the next 90 days a clear win for the business?

## Current delivery and pricing

13. What is your standard setup fee right now? If it varies, give the range.
14. What is your standard retainer right now? If it varies, give the range.
15. Do you have a documented delivery process, or is each build improvised? One sentence.

## Help available

16. Do you have any contractors, VAs, or part-time help today? If yes, what do they do.
17. Have you ever hired or worked closely with a sales setter? If yes, what worked or did not.

## Operating preferences

18. Anything else the coach should know? Constraints, blockers, recent failures, anything that would change the recommendation.
