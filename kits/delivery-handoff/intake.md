# Delivery and Handoff Kit Intake

Answer in your own words. Two sentences each is fine. The coach reads
this and tunes every checklist and the handoff doc to your build. Save
your answers to `intake-filled.md` (not committed).

## The build

1. What did you build, in one plain sentence? Describe it the way the
   owner would, not with tool names.
2. Who is it for? Use a placeholder like [Client Co] and the type of
   business and rough size.
3. How will the client actually use it? What do they see, where do they
   see it, and when?

## How it runs and how it fails

4. When does it run? A specific schedule (day, time, timezone) or the
   trigger if it is event-based.
5. What could break, and how would the client notice? The exact failure
   signal, for example the output not arriving by a certain time, or
   numbers looking wrong.

## Handoff and operations

6. Who is the client contact, the person you hand this to and email
   when something changes? First name and role is enough.
7. What does the monthly retainer cover, and what is explicitly not
   covered without a separate scope? If you are not sure, the coach
   will suggest the standard list.
8. What is the handoff timeline? When does the setup invoice clear,
   when is the walkthrough call, when does it go live?

## Money and access

9. Setup fee and monthly retainer for this build? Taught range is 1K to
   2.5K setup plus 300 to 600 per month. Placeholder is fine.
10. What access do you still need from the client to finish, for
    example read access to a spreadsheet, a sample of the input data,
    or an internal point person?
