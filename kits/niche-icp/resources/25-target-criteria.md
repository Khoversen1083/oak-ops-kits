# 25-Target List Criteria (Week 1)

Build this list before sending a single email. Score every row with the ICP rubric, drop the lowest-scoring 5 to 8, replace them, then send.

## Columns to track

Keep these columns identical whether you work in a CSV or a Google Sheet:

`#, Company, Owner/GM, Title, LinkedIn URL, Email, Phone, Revenue est, Headcount, ICP Score, Pain hypothesis, Sent?, Replied?, Status`

## Sourcing in 90 minutes

1. LinkedIn Sales Navigator (preferred): filter by industry, headcount 50 to 500, and geography. Pull 50 companies, narrow to 25 owner-operated.
2. Google Maps plus filters: if your niche is location-bound (manufacturers, contractors, food businesses), Google Maps with the industry term reveals 100-plus in any metro. Cross-reference website and LinkedIn.
3. Industry directories: every B2B niche has a trade association directory. Find it. Members are owner-operated by default.
4. Competitor lists: when you find one perfect target, check "Other companies you might know" on LinkedIn. Usually competitors and peers in the niche.

## Email finding

Tools, cheapest first:

- Hunter.io free tier: 25 searches per month, decent accuracy.
- Apollo.io free tier: 50 credits, good for verified emails on LinkedIn profiles.
- Manual: `[firstname]@[domain]` works for owner-operated businesses about 60 percent of the time. If it bounces, try `[firstname].[lastname]@[domain]`.

Do not buy a leads database. They are stale and you will get blocklisted.

## What "pain hypothesis" means

One sentence per row. Specific.

- Good: "Weekly inventory count takes 12-plus hours, manually entered into QuickBooks."
- Good: "Sales reps still updating a shared Google Sheet, two-day lag on numbers."
- Bad: "They probably need automation." Too generic. Drop the row.

If you cannot write a specific hypothesis after 5 minutes of research, your ICP score is probably wrong. Re-score.

## Bring to the Week 1 live call

- 25 rows filled
- ICP scores filled for all 25
- Pain hypotheses filled for all 25
- The 5 to 10 lowest-scored rows already replaced
