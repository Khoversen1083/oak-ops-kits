# ICP Scoring Rubric (Week 1)

Use this to score each business on your 25-target list. Anything scoring under 6 out of 10 should not get an email.

## Score each target on these 5 dimensions, 0 to 2 each

| Dimension | 0 points | 1 point | 2 points |
|---|---|---|---|
| Owner-operated | Public, PE-owned, or VC-backed | Family-owned but multi-generation with a non-owner CEO | Founder still runs it day to day |
| Revenue size | Under 10M or over 300M | 10 to 20M or 200 to 300M | 20M to 200M |
| Headcount | Under 30 or over 800 | 30 to 50 or 500 to 800 | 50 to 500 |
| Tech stack signal | Modern stack (HubSpot, Salesforce, Notion all visible) | Some modern, mostly legacy | Spreadsheets plus email plus maybe QuickBooks |
| Pain signal | Polished website, no operations content | Some signs of growth pain | Clear operations strain (hiring posts, founder posting about busy seasons, "we are swamped" reviews) |

Total possible: 10. Threshold to email: 6 or higher.

## Where to find signals (5 minutes per target)

- Owner-operated: LinkedIn. Find the founder, check the title. Bonus if it reads "Founder and CEO since [year]" with a 20-plus year tenure.
- Revenue: Google `[company name] revenue`, ZoomInfo, Owler. For a private company with no public number, infer from headcount times roughly 200K per employee as a sanity check.
- Headcount: LinkedIn company page, People count. Round up since LinkedIn under-counts.
- Tech stack signal: Look at job postings. Hiring an "Excel ninja" or "data entry specialist" is 2 points. Hiring a "Salesforce admin" is 0 points.
- Pain signal: Skim reviews for "long hours," "overworked," "still using spreadsheets." Check the founder's LinkedIn. Do they post about operations? Do they share whiteboard photos?

## Use the rubric BEFORE outreach, not during

Score all 25 targets before sending the first email. The lowest-scoring 8 to 10 are wasted opportunities. Pull them off the list and find replacements.

## Edge cases

- Multi-generation family business with an adult-child CEO: still owner-operated. 2 points.
- Founder retired but still on the board, kid runs it: 1 point (slow to decide).
- Recent PE acquisition (within 18 months): 0 points. They are being optimized for sale and will not pay you.
- Has an "AI Director" or "Director of Innovation": 0 points on tech stack signal. They handle it internally.

## What to do with the score

| Score | Action |
|---|---|
| 9 to 10 | Personalize hard. Spend 20 minutes on this email. |
| 7 to 8 | Standard email, second variant. |
| 6 | Standard email, first variant. |
| 0 to 5 | Drop from list. |

Bring your scored 25-target list to the Week 1 live call.
