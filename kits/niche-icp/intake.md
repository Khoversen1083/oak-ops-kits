# Niche-ICP Kit Intake

Answer in your own words. Two sentences each is fine. The coach reads
this and personalizes every recommendation. Save your answers to
`intake-filled.md` (not committed).

## Your starting point

1. What do you sell today, in plain words (the deliverable, not the marketing)?
2. Who has bought from you so far (industry, role, company size)? Skip if
   nobody has bought yet, and explain who you've been pitching.
3. What is the niche you are testing this week, if any? If unsure, leave blank.

## Three candidate niches

4. Niche A: industry + role + size band (e.g. "small accounting firms, 5-15
   employees, owner-operated, $1M-$5M revenue").
5. Why Niche A is interesting in one sentence (what pain or opportunity).
6. Niche B: same format.
7. Why Niche B is interesting.
8. Niche C: same format.
9. Why Niche C is interesting.

## The owner-operated test

10. For each niche A/B/C, is the buyer the owner of the business? Yes / No /
    Mixed for each.

## Revenue band + recurring-margin test

11. Revenue band per niche (under $1M, $1M-$5M, $5M-$20M, $20M-$200M, $200M+).
12. Does each niche have recurring-margin work that an automation could shrink
    (monthly billing, repeat onboarding, scheduled reporting)?

## Decision criteria

13. Which one criterion will most determine your pick (speed to first client,
    fit with your existing skills, market size, low competition, ease of
    targeting)? One sentence.
14. Anything else the coach should know about your voice, geography, or
    constraints?
