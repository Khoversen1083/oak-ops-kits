# Week 1 Niche and ICP Kit

A downloadable kit for Oak Ops students. Claude does the heavy lifting. You answer questions and review the result.

## Run it in 5 steps

1. Unzip this folder.
2. Open the folder in Claude Code.
3. Say: "help me get started."
4. Answer the 10 questions one at a time.
5. Review your three output files in `outputs/`.

## What is in this folder

- `LESSON.md`: the student lesson. Start here if you want the full context.
- `CLAUDE.md`: instructions Claude reads automatically. You do not need to open this.
- `resources/`: the niche filter, ICP rubric, and target-list criteria Claude reads. Read-only.
- `examples/filled-example.md`: what a strong result looks like.
- `outputs/`: where Claude writes your three files.

## What you get

- `outputs/niche-decision.md`
- `outputs/your-icp-rubric.md`
- `outputs/target-list.csv`

## After you run it

Copy the submit block at the bottom of `outputs/niche-decision.md` into the Skool lesson comments. Bring your locked niche to the Week 1 live call.
