# Claude Code First Automation Kit Intake

Answer in your own words. Two sentences each is fine. The coach reads
this and personalizes every step. Save your answers to
`intake-filled.md` (not committed).

## Your client

1. Who is the client this automation is for? (company, the one
   person you actually deliver to, the relationship status: paying,
   pilot, friend-of-a-friend trial)
2. What do they do? One sentence on the business so the coach has
   context.
3. What does the workflow you are eyeing actually touch? (their
   inbox, a spreadsheet, a CRM, a folder of files, a SaaS account)

## The workflow

4. Name 2 to 4 candidate workflows the client does manually right
   now. Each one should take 30 minutes or more per occurrence and
   repeat weekly or more often.
5. For your top pick, who runs it today? (the client themselves, an
   assistant, a junior person, a contractor)
6. What breaks when this workflow goes wrong? (missed appointments,
   a delayed report, a customer call dropped, a billing error)

## Your starting point

7. How comfortable are you in the terminal? Brand new, a little, or
   fine. Be honest, it changes how the coach writes the build
   steps.
8. Is Claude Code installed and running on the machine you will
   build on? (yes, not yet, installed but never used)
9. What tools or accounts does the client already use that this
   automation might touch? List the names (Gmail, Notion, Sheets,
   HubSpot, Calendly, a custom internal app).

## Your operating preferences

10. Anything else the coach should know? Past failures with
    automation projects, client sensitivities, time constraints,
    data they cannot let leave their network.
