# Monthly Maintenance Kit Intake

Answer in your own words. Two sentences each is fine. The coach reads
this and personalizes every audit, summary email, and next-month plan.
Save your answers to `intake-filled.md` (not committed).

## Your retainer book

1. List your current retainer clients, one per line. For each, include
   the client name, the buyer's first name, and what they pay you per
   month.

2. For each client, what is the retainer scope in plain words? One
   line per client. Example: "monthly tuning of the lead-routing
   automation, on-call for failures, one small build per quarter."

3. For each client, when did they start? Approximate month is fine.

## Your operating rhythm

4. What day of the month do you want to run the monthly review on?
   (Default: last business day of the month.)

5. How do you currently track automation runs and errors per client?
   (Logs, a sheet, a dashboard, rough notes, nothing yet.)

6. Have you ever sent a monthly recap email to a retainer client
   before? If yes, what worked or did not work?

## Your business posture

7. Across all clients, what is the one improvement type you are most
   prepared to ship as an upsell next quarter? (A second automation, a
   dashboard, a custom report, a Slack bot, etc.)

8. What is your typical upsell price band? (Taught range is one-time
   setup 1K to 2.5K and added retainer 100 to 300 per month, you
   choose.)

## Your voice

9. How formal is the relationship with each client? (Lowercase texts,
   formal emails, mixed.) One line per client.

10. Anything else the coach should know about how you want monthly
    emails to read? (Length, tone, signoff, anything you have
    promised the client about cadence.)
