# Example: intake-filled.md (worked example)

Fake retainer book: 3 clients, 2 vet clinics and 1 small accounting
firm. Used in `example-monthly-audit.md` and
`example-client-summary-email.md`.

## Your retainer book

1. **Current retainer clients.**
   - Lakeside Animal Hospital, buyer Dr. Sarah Pham, 400 per month.
   - Eastside Vet Clinic, buyer Dr. Michael Tran, 350 per month.
   - Carrington CPA, buyer Lisa Carrington, 500 per month.

2. **Retainer scope per client.**
   - Lakeside: monthly tuning of the appointment-reminder and
     post-visit follow-up automation, on-call for failures, one small
     improvement per quarter.
   - Eastside: monthly tuning of the same reminder workflow, on-call
     for failures, no improvements baked in.
   - Carrington: monthly tuning of the client intake and document
     routing automation, on-call for failures, quarterly review.

3. **Start dates.**
   - Lakeside: November 2025.
   - Eastside: February 2026.
   - Carrington: January 2026.

## Your operating rhythm

4. **Monthly review day.** Last business day of the month, blocked on
   the calendar from 1pm to 4pm Central.

5. **How runs and errors are tracked.** Lakeside and Eastside both
   write run logs to a Google Sheet I own. Carrington writes to a
   Supabase table with a simple dashboard. All three send me a
   Slack alert on any failure.

6. **Monthly recap history.** Sent ad-hoc recaps to Lakeside twice,
   buyer liked them but said they wanted them on a real cadence.
   Nothing sent yet for Eastside or Carrington.

## Your business posture

7. **One upsell type ready to ship.** A small weekly summary email or
   dashboard for any client that does not have one yet. Two days of
   work, 800 to 1,500 one-time plus 100 to 150 added retainer.

8. **Upsell price band.** One-time 1,500 to 2,500, added retainer 150
   to 300 per month.

## Your voice

9. **Relationship formality.**
   - Lakeside: lowercase texts, casual. Sarah and I have done 3
     in-person visits.
   - Eastside: mixed, polite emails, Michael is the more formal of
     the three.
   - Carrington: formal emails only, Lisa runs a CPA firm and
     expects business writing.

10. **Anything else.** Carrington's renewal date is January, want to
    pre-position any upsell by November. Lakeside referred a Twin
    Cities clinic last month, that referral should show up in the
    May internal plan as a trigger.
