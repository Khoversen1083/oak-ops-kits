## Why this email works

- Recap quotes his exact words ("the Monday morning schedule"), not
  a cleaned-up version
- Value hook ("selling again" + lost 200K bid) is in paragraph 1
  before the labor hours, anchors above the spreadsheet
- Specific day commitment (Tuesday, not "this week") moves the deal
- Invitation to correct ("hit reply if anything landed different")
  surfaces objections before they harden into a no
- No price quoted, no calendar link inside the body, no AI jargon,
  no em dashes
