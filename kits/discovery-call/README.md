# Discovery Call Kit

A pre-call brief, a live discovery script, and a post-call summary email,
written for the prospect on your calendar, ready to run. Operationalizes
the discovery call beats from the Oak Ops sales playbook.

## What this kit does

You open it in Claude Code, answer a short setup interview once, and then
for each booked discovery call it walks you through three phases: research
the prospect and write a one-page brief, run a live call with a custom
6-question script in front of you, and ship a recap email within 30
minutes that restates the pain and proposes a next step.

## Before / After

| Without kit | With kit |
|---|---|
| Show up to the call cold, wing the questions | Open the call with a real observation about their business |
| Forget the dollar number an hour after the call | Pain quantified live, written into the recap |
| Send "great chatting" the next day, lose momentum | Recap and proposed scope sent inside 30 minutes |
| Pitch features when the prospect goes quiet | 6-question framework keeps you in discovery, not in demo |

## Install in 3 steps

```bash
git clone https://github.com/Khoversen1083/oak-ops-kits.git
cd oak-ops-kits/kits/discovery-call
claude
```

Then type: `run the kit`

## What you'll have when you're done

- A one-page pre-call brief in `outputs/<prospect>-brief-<date>.md`
- A prospect-specific live call script with the 6 questions, the recap
  beat, and the close, in `outputs/<prospect>-script-<date>.md`
- A ready-to-send post-call summary email in
  `outputs/<prospect>-summary-<date>.md`
- A mid-call objection bank you can keep open on a second monitor
- A repeatable 3-phase rhythm you run for every booked call going forward

## What you need

- [Claude Code](https://claude.com/claude-code) installed
- A locked niche (one industry, one buyer profile)
- A simple offer you can describe in one sentence
- A booked discovery call on the calendar
- 45 minutes the first time, 20 minutes per call after that

## How long does it take

- One-time setup interview: 5 minutes
- Per call, pre-call brief: 10 minutes
- Per call, live script prep: 10 minutes
- Per call, post-call summary email: 10 minutes (within 30 min of hang-up)
