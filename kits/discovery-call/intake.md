# Discovery Call Kit Intake

Answer in your own words. Two sentences each is fine. The coach reads
this once and personalizes every brief, script, and summary email going
forward. Save your answers to `intake-filled.md` (not committed).

## Your business

1. What do you sell? (the deliverable, not the marketing)
2. Who do you sell to right now? Be specific, industry, role, company
   size.
3. What is the niche you are running discovery calls in this month?

## Your offer

4. What is the one-sentence outcome you promise? (the result, not the
   mechanism)
5. What is the setup fee range you quote? (taught range is 1K to 2.5K,
   you choose)
6. What is the monthly retainer range you quote? (taught range is 300
   to 600, you choose)

## Your discovery framework

7. Do you have a custom question set, or do you want to use the default
   6-question framework in `resources/six-question-framework.md`? If
   custom, paste the questions and the coach will adapt them.

## Your operating preferences

8. What is your calendar link? (Calendly, Cal.com, paste the URL)
9. What is your business email? (the one you send the recap from)
10. What time zone do you operate in?
11. What is your typical close-of-call next step? (send a written
    scope, book a second call, send a proposal, other)
12. Anything else the coach should know about your voice, your niche,
    or constraints?
