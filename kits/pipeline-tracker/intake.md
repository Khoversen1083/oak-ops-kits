# Pipeline Tracker Kit Intake

Answer in your own words. Two sentences each is fine. The coach reads
this and personalizes the tracker, the standup, and the weekly review.
Save your answers to `intake-filled.md` (not committed).

## Your business

1. What is the niche you are tracking pipeline for this week? (one
   industry, one buyer profile)
2. What is the deliverable you sell to them? (the build, not the
   marketing)
3. What is the outcome you promise in week 1?

## Your pipeline

4. Roughly how many active prospects do you have right now? (rows you
   would put in the tracker today)
5. Where do they live today? (spreadsheet, Notion table, inbox, LinkedIn
   DMs, scattered, nothing yet)
6. Do you want custom stage names? (default 14 stages cover most sales
   motions, only rename if your team already speaks a different
   vocabulary)

## Your operating preferences

7. What is your calendar link? (Calendly, Cal.com, paste the URL, the
   standup uses it to draft "book a call" replies)
8. What is your business email? (the one you send from)
9. What time zone do you operate in? (used to compute "days since last
   touch" against your local day)
10. Do you want the global `pipeline status` skill installed to
    `~/.claude/skills/`? (yes lets you say "pipeline status" from any
    folder, no keeps it scoped to this kit folder only)
