# Pricing and Retainer Kit

A proposal-ready pricing sheet for your next build. Setup fee, monthly
retainer, scope language that prevents creep. Operationalizes the
Revenue Playbook pricing lesson.

## What this kit does

You open it in Claude Code, answer 10 intake questions once, describe
the build you are quoting, and walk out with a defensible setup fee,
a monthly retainer that matches the ongoing work, and the verbatim
scope-control language to drop into the proposal.

## Before / After

| Without kit | With kit |
|---|---|
| Pull a number out of the air and undercharge | Walk a decision tree to a defensible price |
| Quote a retainer that does not cover your time | Match retainer to actual monthly work |
| Loose scope, client expects everything for free | Verbatim scope language a buyer cannot misread |
| Freeze on "can you knock it down" replies | Pre-written objection responses, no discount panic |

## Install in 3 steps

```bash
git clone https://github.com/Khoversen1083/oak-ops-kits.git
cd oak-ops-kits/kits/pricing-retainer
claude
```

Then type: `run the kit`

## What you'll have when you're done

- A pricing decision for this specific build, with the reasoning, in
  `outputs/pricing-decision-<date>.md`
- A proposal-ready pricing section you paste straight into the doc, in
  `outputs/proposal-pricing-section-<date>.md`
- An objection card pre-loaded with your numbers, ready for the next
  reply, in `outputs/objection-card-<date>.md`
- A scope-control phrase bank you keep open while the proposal is in
  back-and-forth

## What you need

- [Claude Code](https://claude.com/claude-code) installed
- One build you are about to quote (real prospect, not hypothetical)
- A sense of the prospect's revenue band and how acute the pain is
- 20 minutes for the first run

## How long does it take

- First pricing decision: 20 minutes
- Proposal section plus scope language: 10 minutes after the decision
- Re-run for the next build: 10 minutes total, intake stays filled

## Taught range

Setup fees in this kit run $1,000 to $2,500. Monthly retainers run
$300 to $600. These are the operator-track defaults. The kit will push
you to the top of the range when the pain is acute, the prospect is
above $2M in revenue, or you carry meaningful proof in their niche.
