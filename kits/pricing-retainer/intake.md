# Pricing and Retainer Kit Intake

Answer in your own words. Two sentences each is fine. The coach reads
this and personalizes every pricing decision. Save your answers to
`intake-filled.md` (not committed).

## Your business

1. What do you sell? (the deliverable, not the marketing)
2. Who do you sell to right now? Be specific, industry, role, company
   size.
3. What is the niche you are pricing this build inside?

## Your pricing history

4. What have you charged for setup on past builds? (numbers and a
   one-line note on whether you regret the price)
5. What have you charged for monthly retainer on past builds? (same,
   numbers and regret/win note)
6. What is your default hourly rate when you do change-request work
   outside scope?

## Your current quote

7. What is the workflow this build replaces? One sentence.
8. What is the prospect's revenue band? (under $1M, $1M to $2M, $2M
   to $5M, $5M+)
9. How acute is the pain, 1 to 5? (1 = nice to have, 5 = bleeding,
   have already tried two vendors)

## Operating preferences

10. Anything else the coach should know about your voice, your buyer,
    or your usual constraints?
