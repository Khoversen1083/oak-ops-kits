## Pricing

**Setup: $2,000.**

Covers:
- The build of the dispatcher route plus ETA-confirmation automation,
  end to end
- ServiceTitan and Twilio integration, including the rule logic for
  job-to-tech assignment
- One round of revisions to the dispatch rules before go-live, plus a
  30-minute walkthrough for Carl and the dispatcher at go-live

**Monthly: $400.**

Covers:
- Monitoring the failure logs, I see breakage before the dispatcher
  does
- Fixes when ServiceTitan or Twilio change their API or rename an
  endpoint
- One adjustment per month to the dispatcher rules as the route mix
  shifts

## Out of scope

Out of scope for this engagement:

- Any net-new automation beyond the dispatcher route plus ETA
  confirmation. Scoped separately.
- Changes to systems not listed in Section 2 of this proposal
  (QuickBooks integration, customer review automation, marketing
  campaigns).
- Building a custom dashboard or operator UI beyond the SMS
  notifications and the ServiceTitan-side status updates.
- Training new dispatchers or field techs on the system beyond the
  single 30-minute walkthrough at go-live.
- Data cleanup, customer-history migration, or historical backfill
  from before the go-live date.

Anything in the list above is quoted separately at $150/hour, billed
in 2-hour minimum blocks. I send a quote, you approve, then I start.

## If the business changes

If Desert Air needs a new workflow automated, say the customer review
follow-up loop, or a different system integrated, that is a new
engagement. I will scope and price it separately, the same way I
scoped this one. I do not bundle net-new work into the retainer. This
keeps your monthly cost predictable.

## Payment terms

Setup is invoiced in two parts. $1,000 on signature, $1,000 on
go-live. The monthly retainer starts the day after go-live and is
billed monthly in advance. Net 7 on all invoices.

## Cancellation

The retainer is month to month. You can cancel any month with 30 days
notice. On cancellation, the automation keeps running on your
infrastructure, you keep all the access. I stop monitoring and stop
fixing on the cancellation date.

## The math, plain

A second dispatcher is roughly $60,000 per year fully loaded. Setup
plus the first 6 months of retainer is $4,400. That is 7 percent of a
dispatcher-year. If the automation covers what one dispatcher day
covers, the math is straightforward.
