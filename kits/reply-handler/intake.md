# Reply Handler Kit Intake

Answer in your own words. Two sentences each is fine. The coach reads
this and personalizes every reply template. Save your answers to
`intake-filled.md` (not committed).

If you already ran the Cold Email kit, copy the matching answers from
that intake. Same niche, same offer, same calendar.

## Your business and niche

1. What is the niche you are targeting right now? (industry, role,
   company size, one sentence)
2. What is the pain hypothesis you are testing with cold email this
   week? (one sentence, the specific workflow you think is eating
   their team's time)

## Your offer

3. What is the setup fee range you are quoting? (taught range is 1K
   to 2.5K)
4. What is the monthly retainer range you are quoting? (taught range
   is 300 to 600)
5. What is the one concrete result you have, your own or a prior
   buyer's, that you can lead with in a reply?

## Your operating preferences

6. What is your calendar link? (Calendly, Cal.com, paste the URL)
7. What is your business email? (the one prospects are replying to)
8. What is your first name as you sign emails?
9. What time zone do you operate in? (so the coach knows what
   "business hours" means for the 4-hour reply rule)
10. Anything else the coach should know about your voice when
    responding to replies? (formal vs casual, any phrases you avoid,
    any niche-specific norms)
