# Cold Email Kit Intake

Answer in your own words. Two sentences each is fine. The coach reads
this and personalizes every template. Save your answers to
`intake-filled.md` (not committed).

## Your business

1. What do you sell? (the deliverable, not the marketing)
2. Who do you sell to right now? Be specific, industry, role, company
   size.
3. What is the niche you are testing this week?

## Your offer

4. What is the setup fee? (taught range is 1K to 2.5K, you choose)
5. What is the monthly retainer? (taught range is 300 to 600, you
   choose)
6. What is the outcome you promise in week 1? Be concrete.

## Your proof

7. What is a result you have gotten for yourself or one prior buyer?
   Numbers if possible.
8. What is a credential, story, or hook that makes you believable in
   this niche?

## Your operating preferences

9. What is your calendar link? (Calendly, Cal.com, paste the URL)
10. What is your business email? (the one you will send from)
11. What time zone do you operate in?
12. Anything else the coach should know about your voice or
    constraints?
