# Cold Email Kit

Personalized cold emails plus a 3-touch follow-up sequence plus a reply
decision tree, written for your niche, ready to send. Operationalizes
lessons 6, 7, 8 of the Lead Acquisition Playbook.

## What this kit does

You open it in Claude Code, answer 12 intake questions once, paste 5
sample prospects, and walk out with 5 ready-to-send cold emails, the
full follow-up sequence for each, and a reply triage card you keep open
while the inbox fills up.

## Before / After

| Without kit | With kit |
|---|---|
| Stare at a blank email for 20 minutes | Five drafts in 10 minutes |
| Generic "I came across your company" opener | Specific opener per prospect, pulled from research |
| Forget to follow up, then forget the thread | 3-touch sequence drafted on day 0 |
| Freeze on a "what does it cost" reply | Pre-written response, in your voice |

## Install in 3 steps

```bash
git clone https://github.com/Khoversen1083/oak-ops-kits.git
cd oak-ops-kits/kits/cold-email
claude
```

Then type: `run the kit`

## What you'll have when you're done

- 5 personalized cold emails ready to send, in `outputs/cold-emails-<date>.md`
- 3-touch follow-up sequence (Day 3, Day 8, Day 14, breakup) for each of the 5 prospects
- A reply decision tree wired to your niche and your voice, ready to keep open in a tab
- A subject-line bank with 4 tested variants for your niche
- A pre-send checklist so you actually send instead of revising forever

## What you need

- [Claude Code](https://claude.com/claude-code) installed
- A locked niche (one industry, one buyer profile)
- A simple offer (setup fee plus retainer, you choose the numbers)
- 30 minutes for the first run

## How long does it take

- First cold email batch: 30 minutes
- Follow-up sequence: 10 minutes after the cold email batch
- Reply decision tree: 10 minutes
- Re-run for a new niche later: 15 minutes total, because intake is already filled
