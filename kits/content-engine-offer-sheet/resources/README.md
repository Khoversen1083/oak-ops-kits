# Resources

Source-of-truth files the coach reads while running the kit. Read-only,
not student-editable. If you need to change the template, fork the
repo.

| File | What it is |
|---|---|
| `voice-rules.md` | The hard and soft voice rules the coach enforces. Includes 5 worked bad-vs-good examples for content. |
| `anchor-piece-template.md` | The weekly anchor structure for LinkedIn post or Loom outline. Hook, build, proof, soft close. |
| `derivatives-template.md` | How to chop the anchor into 3 short-form posts for secondary surfaces. |
| `lead-magnet-template.md` | The one-page lead magnet outline plus a monthly refresh checklist. |
| `offer-sheet-template.md` | The single-page services plus pricing plus calendar offer sheet. |
| `content-calendar-template.md` | The 4-week rolling grid for anchor, derivatives, and magnet refresh. |

The coach reads these in this order during a run:

1. `voice-rules.md` at Step 1
2. `anchor-piece-template.md` at Step 2
3. `derivatives-template.md` at Step 3
4. `lead-magnet-template.md` at Step 4
5. `offer-sheet-template.md` at Step 5
6. `content-calendar-template.md` at Step 6
