# 4-week content calendar template

A rolling grid so you always know what is shipping this week and next.
Updated every Friday after the anchor is drafted. Lives in
`outputs/content-calendar-<date>.md`, regenerated weekly.

## Structure

A simple grid with 4 weeks, 7 days each, populated with the anchor,
derivatives, lead magnet refresh, and one placeholder for next week's
anchor draft. Anything you are not posting that day stays blank.

## Variables to fill

- `<topic>`: the anchor topic for the week, one short phrase
- Surface columns: match the user's intake (primary plus 2
  secondaries)
- Refresh date: first business day of the month

## How to use it

- Print or pin this calendar somewhere visible
- Every Friday after the client call, draft next week's anchor and
  fill in the topic
- Every Tuesday morning, post the anchor, then queue the 3
  derivatives
- First Monday of every month, refresh the lead magnet (use
  `lead-magnet-template.md`)
- Move "do not check the inbox" days (Sat, Sun) into recovery, the
  engine runs without you on weekends

## What NOT to do

- Do not pre-schedule a month of derivatives. The proof line gets
  stale within 2 weeks because something more recent will come up.
- Do not skip the Friday draft window. Sunday night drafting is the
  trap that kills most content engines.
- Do not add more than 3 derivatives per anchor. The fourth one is
  always weaker than the third.

## Cadence

- Calendar regenerated every Friday by re-running this kit (Step 6
  only, the rest of the outputs persist)
- After 4 weeks, the rolling grid is a steady habit. After 12 weeks
  the engine is producing inbound. Trust the cadence.
