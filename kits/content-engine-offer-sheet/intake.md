# Content Engine and Offer Sheet Kit Intake

Answer in your own words. Two sentences each is fine. The coach reads
this and personalizes every template. Save your answers to
`intake-filled.md` (not committed).

## Your business

1. What do you sell? (the deliverable, not the marketing)
2. Who do you sell to right now? Be specific, industry, role, company
   size.
3. What is the locked niche the content engine is pointing at?

## Your offer

4. What is the setup fee? (taught range is 1K to 2.5K, you choose)
5. What is the monthly retainer? (taught range is 300 to 600, you
   choose)

## Your content surface

6. What is your primary content surface this quarter? (LinkedIn,
   Loom-to-YouTube, IG reels, Twitter. Pick one.)
7. What are the 2 secondary surfaces you will repurpose to? (pick from
   the same list, ranked in priority order)
8. How often are you posting on the primary surface today? Be honest.

## Your operating preferences

9. What is your calendar link? (Calendly, Cal.com, paste the URL)
10. What is your business email and any voice samples or constraints
    the coach should know about?
