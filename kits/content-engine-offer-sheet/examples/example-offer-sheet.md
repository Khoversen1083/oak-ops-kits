## Vet Clinic Content Engine

The outcome
A steady stream of owner-vet conversations in your DMs, started by
the buyer, not chased by you. Posts that sound like an operator who
has actually run a clinic, not an agency that found you on Apollo.

Who this is for
Owner-vets running single-location clinics, 2 to 6 vets, who have
tried doing their own content and dropped it after 3 weeks because
Sunday nights kept eating the draft.

What you get
- One LinkedIn anchor a week, drafted in your voice, sized 150 to
  250 words, posted Tuesday morning
- 3 short-form derivatives chopped from that anchor, posted across
  Threads and Twitter over the following 3 days
- One monthly lead magnet refresh, a one-pager that solves a specific
  clinic pain and routes to your calendar
- A 4-week rolling content calendar so you always know what is next

Cadence
Anchor lands Tuesday 7 to 9am Central. Derivatives roll Wednesday,
Thursday. Lead magnet refreshed first business day of every month.
Weekly readout sent every Friday with what shipped and what is next.

Setup fee
1,500. Covers the niche lock, voice calibration, the first month of
content, the first lead magnet build, and the calendar setup.

Monthly retainer
400 per month. Covers ongoing anchor plus 3 derivatives per week
plus the monthly magnet refresh plus the Friday readout.

What is not included
- Paid ad spend or boosted posts
- Full video editing, motion graphics, or podcast production
- Website builds, landing pages, or funnel software

How it runs
The first 30 days are a pilot. I build the engine with you and run
it weekly. At day 30 we decide together to continue on retainer,
scope a new project, or end clean. No long-term lock-in. Setup paid
before kickoff, retainer billed monthly in advance.
