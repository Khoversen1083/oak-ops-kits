# Content Engine and Offer Sheet Kit

A weekly content engine that drives inbound leads, plus the one-page
offer sheet that closes them. You ship 1 anchor piece, 3 short-form
derivatives, and 1 monthly lead magnet, all routed back to your
calendar through a single document.

## What this kit does

You open it in Claude Code, answer 10 intake questions once, and walk
out with a 4-week content calendar, a written anchor piece, 3
short-form posts chopped from that anchor, a refreshed lead magnet,
and a one-page offer sheet you can send a warm lead in under a minute.

## Before / After

| Without kit | With kit |
|---|---|
| Stare at LinkedIn for an hour, post nothing | Anchor drafted in 20 minutes, in your voice |
| Post once, lose energy, ghost for 3 weeks | 4-week rolling calendar, you know what is next |
| One-off lead magnet from 2 years ago | Monthly refresh that ties back to a paid offer |
| "Can you send me what you do?" answered with a 4-paragraph email | One-page offer sheet, calendar link at the bottom |

## Install in 3 steps

```bash
git clone https://github.com/Khoversen1083/oak-ops-kits.git
cd oak-ops-kits/kits/content-engine-offer-sheet
claude
```

Then type: `run the kit`

## What you'll have when you're done

- 1 anchor piece, ready to post on LinkedIn or shoot as a Loom, in
  `outputs/anchor-piece-<date>.md`
- 3 short-form derivatives chopped from the anchor, in
  `outputs/derivatives-<date>.md`
- 1 lead magnet outline plus a refresh checklist, in
  `outputs/lead-magnet-<date>.md`
- 1 offer sheet, single page, services plus pricing plus calendar, in
  `outputs/offer-sheet-<date>.md`
- A 4-week content calendar populated with the above, in
  `outputs/content-calendar-<date>.md`

## What you need

- [Claude Code](https://claude.com/claude-code) installed
- A locked niche (one industry, one buyer profile)
- A simple offer (setup fee plus retainer, you choose the numbers)
- Your calendar URL and the email you send from
- 45 minutes for the first run

## How long does it take

- Anchor piece: 20 minutes
- Derivatives: 10 minutes after the anchor
- Lead magnet outline: 10 minutes
- Offer sheet: 10 minutes
- Re-run weekly for the anchor and derivatives: 20 minutes total
